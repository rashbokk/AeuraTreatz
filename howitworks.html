Good to Know
⏰
Lead Times
Standard cakes need 48 hours notice. Custom, tiered and wedding cakes require 3–5 days. Festival slots (Diwali, Christmas, Valentine's Day) fill up fast — book 2 weeks early.
🔄
Cancellations
Cancellations 48+ hours before baking date: full refund. Within 24–48 hours: 50% refund. Within 24 hours: no refund as ingredients are already sourced and prepared.
📦
Storage Tips
Store in the refrigerator. Remove 30 minutes before serving for best texture. Fresh cream cakes best consumed within 2 days. Fondant cakes within 3 days.
🌱
Allergens
All cakes are eggless. Gluten-free options available on request. Our kitchen handles nuts — please mention all allergies at order time. Cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
