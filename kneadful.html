Our Story

Baking is my
love language.

"Every slice tells a story."

Hello! I'm Rashmi — a home baker based in Bangalore with a deep love for creating celebration cakes that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

My journey started when I couldn't find a truly good eggless cake for my own birthday. So I started baking — experimenting with flavours, techniques, and the finest ingredients I could find. What began as a personal quest turned into something people kept asking me to do for them.

Every Aeura Treatz cake is made fresh to order in my home kitchen, with zero compromise on quality.
